Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction & Scope

Rexon Welds & Tools ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you visit our website or purchase industrial welding equipment, machinery, tools, and accessories from us.

This policy applies to all users of our website, including visitors, customers, and registered members. By using our website or services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

Your Privacy Matters: We respect your privacy and are committed to maintaining the confidentiality of your personal information in accordance with applicable Indian data protection laws.

2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide you with quality service, process your orders, and improve your shopping experience.

2.1 Personal Information

Information you provide directly to us includes:

  • Contact Details: Name, phone number, email address
  • Shipping Address: Delivery address, city, state, PIN code
  • Billing Information: Billing address, GST number (if applicable for business purchases)
  • Account Information: Username, password (encrypted), account preferences
  • Business Details: Company name, business type, industry (for B2B customers)

2.2 Order & Transaction Information

  • Product purchase history and order details
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party payment gateways)
  • Invoice and billing records
  • Warranty registration details
  • Service and support requests

2.3 Technical & Usage Data

Information collected automatically when you visit our website:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type and version, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, click patterns, referral sources
  • Location Data: Approximate geographic location based on IP address
  • Cookies & Tracking: Data collected through cookies and similar technologies

2.4 Communication Records

  • Customer service inquiries and correspondence
  • Chat transcripts and support tickets
  • Feedback, reviews, and ratings
  • Email communications and newsletter subscriptions

3. How We Collect Information

3.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information when you:

  • Create an account or register on our website
  • Place an order for products

We may receive information from:

  • Marketing and analytics platforms

5. Cookies Policy

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and provide personalized services. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and analyze website usage.

Types of Cookies We Use:

Cookie Type Purpose
Essential Cookies Necessary for website functionality, including shopping cart and checkout processes
Performance Cookies Collect anonymous data about how visitors use our website to improve performance
Functionality Cookies Remember your preferences and settings for a personalized experience
Marketing Cookies Track your activity to deliver relevant advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness

Managing Cookies: You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality and your user experience.

We may also use third-party cookies from services like Google Analytics to analyze website traffic and user behavior.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted partners in the following circumstances:

6.1 Service Providers

Courier & Logistics Partners:

  • We share delivery addresses and contact details with shipping companies to fulfill your orders
  • These partners are contractually obligated to use your information only for delivery purposes

Payment Gateway Providers:

  • Payment information is processed securely through third-party payment gateways
  • We do not store complete card or banking details on our servers
  • Payment providers comply with industry security standards (PCI-DSS)

6.2 Warranty & Service Partners

  • Product information and purchase details may be shared with manufacturers for warranty registration
  • Customer contact details are provided to authorized service centers for warranty claims and repairs

6.3 Business Operations

  • Technology service providers (hosting, email services, analytics)
  • Customer support platforms and communication tools
  • Marketing and advertising partners (with your consent)

6.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes, court orders, or government requests
  • Enforce our Terms and Conditions
  • Protect against fraud or security threats
  • Defend our legal rights in litigation

7. Data Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Our Security Practices Include:

  • Encryption: Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption for data transmission
  • Secure Servers: Password-protected servers with restricted access
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Monitoring: Continuous monitoring for security vulnerabilities
  • Data Backup: Regular backups to prevent data loss
  • Payment Security: PCI-DSS compliant payment processing
Important: While we take reasonable precautions to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account password.

8. Data Retention Policy

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Retention Periods:

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period after closure
  • Order & Transaction Records: Retained for 7 years for accounting, tax, and warranty purposes
  • Marketing Communications: Until you unsubscribe or request removal
  • Support Records: Retained for 2-3 years for quality assurance and dispute resolution
  • Website Analytics: Anonymous data retained for statistical analysis

After the retention period expires, personal information is securely deleted or anonymized.

9. User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

9.1 Right to Access

You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

9.2 Right to Correction

You can update or correct inaccurate personal information through your account settings or by contacting us.

9.3 Right to Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements.

9.4 Right to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw consent for marketing communications or data processing activities at any time.

9.5 Right to Data Portability

You can request your personal information in a structured, commonly used format for transfer to another service provider.

To Exercise Your Rights: Contact us at privacy@rexonwelds.com with your request. We will respond within 30 days.
